自我介绍 / About Me

Sociocultural Anthropology · Digital & Animated Cultures in Contemporary China

English

I am a prospective PhD student in sociocultural anthropology focusing on digital and animated cultures in contemporary China. My research explores how practices such as kigurumi, cosplay, and vtubing make fictional and animated forms socially present in everyday life, and how these practices shape experiences of belonging, moral worlds, and social relations across online and urban spaces. I am particularly interested in media, infrastructure, performance, and affect.

中文

我是一名拟攻读社会文化人类学博士的研究者，研究当代中国的数字文化与动画文化。 我的研究关注诸如kigurumi、cosplay与vtubing等实践，如何使虚构与动画形式在日常生活中变得“社会性在场”，并由此塑造人们的归属感、道德世界与社会关系，在网络与城市空间之间展开。 我尤其关注媒介、基础设施、表演性与情感等议题。
